About Us

Bridging Cultures,

Dragon AI Media Inc. is the advertising subsidiary of Philippine Dragon Media Network — combining deep cultural intelligence with AI-driven strategies to deliver campaigns that resonate across the Philippines and APAC.

Our Story

Born from a passion for
cultural connection

Dragon AI Media Inc. was founded with a singular vision: to become the most trusted bridge between Chinese-Filipino communities and the wider Philippine market. As the advertising arm of Philippine Dragon Media Network — one of the Philippines' most influential Chinese-language media platforms — we bring unparalleled cultural insight and authentic market access to every campaign we build.

From our roots in cultural media, we have grown into a full-service AI-powered marketing agency, serving over 200 brand partners with creative production, strategic media buying, community activations, and data-driven digital campaigns. Every strategy we craft is informed by deep market intelligence and a genuine understanding of the Filipino and Chinese-Filipino consumer.

Origins
Philippine Dragon Media Network established as a leading Chinese-language news platform in the Philippines, serving the Chinese-Filipino community.
Founded
Dragon AI Media Inc. launched as the dedicated advertising subsidiary, extending reach into full-service brand marketing and strategic media buying.
Growth
Expanded service portfolio to include AI-driven campaign analytics, full-scale video production, and integrated digital strategy across the APAC region.
Today
200+ brand partners across the Philippines and APAC, with campaigns spanning OOH, digital, broadcast, and live community events.
